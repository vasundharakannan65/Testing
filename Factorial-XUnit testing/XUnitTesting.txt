Unit testing:
1) to test small component in a s/w & uses TDD approach
2) small component could be any function, any property or any class which is handling the specific functionality
3)  basically ensures us that the system is designed as per the requirements and help us to figure out the bugs

XUnit testing - advantages,purpose 
1) eliminates the risk of test method dependencies
2) extensible than Nunit & MSunit
3) [fact] attribute is used instead of [test]
4) allows you to create new attributes to control your tests
5) allows you to inherit from Fact, Theory, and other attributes
